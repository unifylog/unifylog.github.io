Some 90Droid users have reported that the only way to access the app is to go to the Android Market and launch it from there.

If this happens to you please do the following:

  1. Open the app (by going to the market),
  2. Backup your workouts (Make sure you get a success message) If backup doesn't work for any reason stop here and send us an email
  3. Uninstall the app
  4. Go to the market and download it again
  5. Go to Restore.

If you follow this instructions you will get the app to work and you won't lose any data. The cause for this problem is a change in the licensing service for Android. UnfortunatelyÂ we don't have control over this within the App. We are sorry for this inconvenience.

Please let us know if you have any questions or problems with this approach.
