How Do I Track X2 With 90Droid?

Jan 2nd, 2012

From the main screen go to Programs > Menu > Download More.

There you’ll find many exercise programs shared by the community, such as P90X2, Hybrids, and many others.

Please note that, P90X2 is a trademark of Beachbody, you’ll need the DVDs for instruction on how to do the exercises. And we are not related to Beachbody in any way.

Enjoy

90Droid v4.6 Released

Oct 2nd, 2011

This version offers a way to enter previous workouts, a frequently requested feature.

To do that simply tap on menu > Show Workout Date, this will set the date for the workout, this date will be persistent so you can enter as many as you need for the same day.

If the workout date is not shown, today’s date will be used as usual.

Enjoy,

Team 90Droid
