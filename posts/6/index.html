90Droid v1.6 Released

Nov 21st, 2010

This release includes the long-requested custom notes. Now you can type your comments on every exercise. Here is how it works:

When you click on the notes button you get this screen where you can type any comment you want

You can also  select any of the quick notes on the list. This will replace whatever was in the text field

Once the notes are saved, they will be displayed in a short format in the previous list. Ellipsis denote text too long to display in that space. Also note that the Date is now displayed in a new column

To read the full-length text:

Long-press over the element in the previous list and select”View Notes”

That will display the notes for that workout instance
