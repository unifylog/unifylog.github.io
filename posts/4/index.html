90Droid Is Not Saving My Workouts

Apr 18th, 2011

Symptom: When you hit save on a workout then hit prev to see the results, you don’t see the item in the history.

Your workout is being saved it’s just not showing in the “previous” list

Solution: go to Cycles and make sure that the active cycle includes today’s date. The easiest way to do this is to leave the end-date empty, that will include everything from the start date on.

We created cycles to help you filter previous workouts without having to delete them. If you set a cycle as active but the end-date does not include today’s date, the history list will not display them. All you will see is a warning.

Addendum: We have created a new post that can give you some other clues: Filtering Records

90Droid Says It’s Not Installed After Update

Apr 15th, 2011

Some 90Droid users have reported that the only way to access the app is to go to the Android Market and launch it from there.

If this happens to you please do the following:

  1. Open the app (by going to the market),
  2. Backup your workouts (Make sure you get a success message) If backup doesn’t work for any reason stop here and send us an email
  3. Uninstall the app
  4. Go to the market and download it again
  5. Go to Restore.

If you follow this instructions you will get the app to work and you won’t lose any data. The cause for this problem is a change in the licensing service for Android. Unfortunately we don’t have control over this within the App. We are sorry for this inconvenience.

Please let us know if you have any questions or problems with this approach.
